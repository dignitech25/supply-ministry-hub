name: Canonical site claims
description: Single source of truth for product count, category breadth, years of experience, and the 24-hour response claim. Use these exact phrasings everywhere.
type: constraint
---
Site-wide canon for marketing claims. Do not invent variations.

- **Product count**: `2,000+ products` (never 468, 4,400, or any other number)
- **Category breadth**: `across all AT categories` or `every assistive technology category`. Never assert a specific category count.
- **Experience**: `15+ years in assistive technology`
- **Speed claim**: `24-hour response on quote requests` ONLY. Never promise 24-hour dispatch, same-day delivery, or 48-hour quote turnaround. Delivery lead times are confirmed per quote.

**Why:** User audited the site 2026-04-26. Speed claim updated from 48-hour to 24-hour response on 2026-04-26 because 24-hour better reflects actual operational responsiveness.

**How to apply:** When writing or editing marketing copy, SEO meta, FAQ answers, testimonials, or hero stats, use the exact phrasings above. If you see `dispatch` or `48-hour` in user-facing copy, rewrite to `24-hour response` or remove the time promise entirely.
