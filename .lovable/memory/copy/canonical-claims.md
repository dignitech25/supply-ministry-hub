name: Canonical site claims
description: Single source of truth for product count, category breadth, years of experience, and 48-hour speed claim. Use these exact phrasings everywhere.
type: constraint
---
Site-wide canon for marketing claims. Do not invent variations.

- **Product count**: `2,000+ products` (never 468, 4,400, or any other number)
- **Category breadth**: `across all AT categories` or `every assistive technology category`. Never assert a specific category count.
- **Experience**: `15+ years in assistive technology`
- **Speed claim**: `48-hour quote turnaround` ONLY. Never promise 48-hour dispatch, same-week delivery, or 24-48h delivery. Delivery lead times are confirmed per quote.

**Why:** User audited the site 2026-04-26 and found contradictory numbers (468 vs 4,400 vs 2,000 products; 3 vs 15 years; "48-hour dispatch" vs "48-hour quotes" used interchangeably).

**How to apply:** When writing or editing marketing copy, SEO meta, FAQ answers, testimonials, or hero stats, use the exact phrasings above. If you see `dispatch` in user-facing copy, rewrite to `quote turnaround` or remove the time promise entirely.
